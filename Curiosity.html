Quotation, Question, Comment #1

Selection: My Quote Question Comment #1 fits the criteria for generating inquiry because I made questions in response to the designated readings. The QQC assignment asks the student to analyze Bitzer’s “Rhetorical Situation” and after responding to the initial prompt, generate a question of them to prompt other students in the discussion to respond to. The generation of the prompt specifical is what I believe made this assignment a perfect fit for the generating inquiry outcome.
